"Find & document (with a reference & one sentence & 1 image) 3 x examples of interesting / engaging / quirky / unique / or otherwise noteworthy PHYSICAL COMPUTING examples. These could be wearables, installations, design or art objects. How are they interactive? What do they do?".

01 LiDAR feature, for DIY ESP32 Watch. Build by RoboticWorx

Source: @roboticworx

A feature used for the DIY ESP32 watch; That allows the user to measure the distance between the watch and any surface, by emiting a lazer from the watch, to hit the desired surface.

02 Shape of Water. Exhibited at the Rossana Orlandi Gallery

Source: @rossana_orlandi

'The installation ‘Shape of Water’ was designed by Katharina Sook Wilting and Moon Seop Seo which shows the caustic effect, optically created spots of light caused by refraction through water. It challenges our perception of water and invites us to reconnect to the philosophical and poetic meaning of water, in contrast to its utilitarian definition in our current economic system.'
