WORKSHOP ACTIVITY 1: Offline Algorithmic thinking

"A Throw of The Dice will never abolish geometry"

"Slave to the Algorithm"

The Task: "Spend a day at the Mercy of Chance. Do 10 Activities that you determine by Dice roll, Coin flip or other 'random' element ~ Document the Rules and the Outcome."

Activity #1:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll snooze my alarm

Rule: Heads- yes, Tails- no

Outcome: Tails.

Activity #2:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll listen to music in the shower

Heads- listen to music, Tails- Don’t listen to music

Outcome: Heads

Activity #3:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll wear blue socks or green socks

Rule: Heads-Blue, Tails- Green

Outcome: Heads.

Activity #4:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll have eggs or cereals for breakfast.

Rule: Heads- Eggs, Tails- Cereal

Outcome: Tails.

Activity #5:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll make myself a drip coffee, or get one from a cafe

Rule: Heads-drip coffee, Tails- cafe

Outcome: Tails.

Activity #6:

I was walking past a café on my way to University. I let a coin-toss decide if I'll get a coffee there, or later at University

Rule: Heads- Coffee at Uni, Tails- Coffee at café here.

Outcome: Heads.

Activity #7:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll study at home after class, or study at uni.

Rule: Heads-Uni, Tails- home

Outcome: Tails.

Activity #8:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll listen to music on the tram home

Rule: Heads- listen to music, Tails- don’t.

Outcome: Tails.

Activity #9:

Letting a coin-toss decide if Ill water my plants now, or in an hour.

Rule: Heads- Water Now, Tails-water in an hour.

Outcome: Heads.

Activity #10:

Letting a coin-toss decide if I'll watch a movie, or not.

Rule: Heads- movie, Tails- no movie.

Outcome: Tails.
